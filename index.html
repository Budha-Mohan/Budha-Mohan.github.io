About me

I am a Research Scientist at Alice & Bob, working on the theory of superconducting circuits and quantum error correction for bosonic codes. Previously, I was a PhD student in the Quantic team of Inria under the supervision of Alain Sarlette and Mazyar Mirrahimi, working on the stabilization and control of cat qubits. During my PhD, I also stayed 4 months in the group led by Alexandre Blais to work on the theory of superconducting devices.

I co-created and maintain Dynamiqs, an open-source library that provides simulation tools for the time-dynamics of quantum systems, based on JAX.

Today, my research interests are broadly on the interplay of superconducting circuits with quantum error correction, with a particular emphasis on model learning, quantum optimal control, and architectures for scalable quantum error correction.
