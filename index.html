About me

I am a Research fellow at Holographic Himalaya, working on Traffic optimization via Random Matrix Theory (RMT). Previously, I was a Fuse AI Fellow at Fusemachines.

Today, my research and project interests are broadly on the interplay of Quantum Machine Learning (QML) with a particular emphasis on model learning, quantum optimal control, and architectures for scalable quantum solutions.
